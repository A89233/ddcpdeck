MotorTrend Car Analysis

Analysis with Shiny Application

Venkat Gullapalli

Synopsis


MotorTrend Car Data Analysis product is built in shiny to do the following:

  • Provide interface to filter cars based on weight and engine size.
  • Analyze mtcars dataset in R, and provide results.
  • Provide reactive results in graphical format and text.
  • User can compare by selecting different cars.

Input/Output

Input

  • Weight of car - Slider to set min and max weight of cars.
  • Engine size of car - Slider to set min and max size of engine of cars.
  • Car from selection - based on Weight and Size of engine.

Output

  • Graph showing mpg vs weight of cars.
  • Graph showing mpg vs engine size of cars.
  • Graph showing speed vs weight of cars.
  • Graph showing speed vs engine size of cars.
  • In all above graphs selected car is shown annotated with a symbol.
  • Conclusion has details of car selected.

Dataset - mtcars

Data consists of - (results below are calculated by R code using mtcars data)

  • 11 (4-cylinder cars), 7 (6-cylinder) cars and 14 (8-cylinder) cars.
  • Fastest car is Ford Pantera L that can go 1/4 mile in 14.5 seconds
  • Slowest car is Merc 230 that can go 1/4 mile in 22.9 seconds
  • Toyota Corolla has the best economy of 33.9 mpg
  • Cadillac Fleetwood, Lincoln Continental has the worst economy of 10.4 mpg
  • Heaviest car at 5424 lbs is Lincoln Continental
  • Lightest car at 1513 lbs is Lotus Europa
  • Cadillac Fleetwood has maximum engine capacity of 472 cubic inches
  • Toyota Corolla has minimum engine capacity of 71.1 cubic inches
